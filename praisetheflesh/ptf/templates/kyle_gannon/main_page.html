About

I'm not great at nor do I like describing myself or my art so let's just start this thing from the beginning...

Art has been a part of me from the start, don't really know how I got into it, none of my family were artists or were even into it so maybe some alien or demonic entity implanted the interest in me, who knows. Most of my first drawings were of dragons, skate board and band logos, and graffiti iconography, anything I thought was cool or badass (because I was the furthest thing from being cool or badass). Entering high school is when I really dived in, taking classes (had an amazing teacher who pushed me and all my buttons) and drawing/painting ALL THE TIME. Artists like Bacon and Beksinski, pretty much any artist who created deranged, psychotic, otherworldly, creepy imagery captured and held my attention hostage. Went to graphic design school after, learning different applications to apply my vision widespread and really cranking down on developing my style, the rest is history.

Numerous things contribute to what inspires me and the reason why I create art. My childhood and upbringing wasn't all sunshine and rainbows so a lot of it come from the darker periods of my life, I feel like those moments had a larger role in forming who I am and using art is how I work out my thoughts and feelings about them. I could be here all day explaining what exactly inspires me but here's the bullet points: my life and experiences, the people around me, other artists, conflict, contrast, comics, video games, sci-fi, fantasy, horror, music, movies, the darker side of humanity, the unknown, the uncanny valley, fear, disgust, mythology, anguish, love, hate, truth, lies, badassery, the grotesque, the macabre and FLESH FLESH FLESH.

I consider myself a private person but an open book to whoever the interested party is so if this description didn't tickle your fancy, feel free to ask away via contact page.
